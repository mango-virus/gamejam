How it works

  1. Fork this repo and clone your fork.
  2. Build your game inside games/<your-id>/. Feed SPEC.md into your agent for the rules and portal protocol.
  3. Enable GitHub Pages on your fork — your game goes live at <you>.github.io/gamejam/games/<your-id>/.
  4. Open a PR adding your entry to games.json. The showcase pulls live deploy status from your fork.
